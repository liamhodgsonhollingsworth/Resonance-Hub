# A game with infinite ways to play

The same architecture can be a philosophical game played in pure thought, or a method of gamifying your actual life with AI assistance developing and optimizing the game alongside you. The system tracks rules, scores, and progressions; the player and the AI together evolve the rules as the game is played. Use it when the goal is structured play, either reflective or applied to lived experience.
