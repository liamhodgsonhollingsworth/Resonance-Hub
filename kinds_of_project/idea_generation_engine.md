# An idea-generation and improvement engine

A tool for finding connections between unrelated fields, evaluating its own outputs against criteria you provide, and explicitly searching for blindspots and suggestions you had not considered. Use it when the goal is structured exploration of an idea space — recombination across domains, self-critique, and surfacing of what you did not think to ask.
